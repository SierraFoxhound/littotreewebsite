About

Greetings, I am Littotree!

I am a digital artist who specializes in environments and characters. I am constantly trying different art styles and learning new methods of art skills. I enjoy creating concept art for my projects, and I am currently working on a video game that is based on my art. My goal is to make art that brings feelings of nostalgia to the young and old!

Feel free to join me on my journey to create!

Please follow my social media to stay up-to-date on my work.
