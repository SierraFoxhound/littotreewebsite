Terms Of Service for Private Commissions

General Terms

  • Prices are subject to change based on demand
  • *Starting prices may change depending on the scale of the commission
  • I have the right to reject any order for any reason

Payment

  • Currency accepted is USD and can be paid through Paypal or any American Payment service such as Zelle or bank transfer.
  • Commission will be paid 50% upfront after have agreed to give you a slot and requested you to send me a payment.
  • Commission will be 100% paid before delivery.
  • Don't send me any payment before I have agreed to give you a slot and requested you to send me a payment.

Process & Delivery

  • The time it takes to finish your commission varies from 1 week - 3 months depending on factors such as health, the complexity of the commissioned piece, or other work.
  • Once the piece is finished you will receive the full-resolution image along with a web-friendly size. Note that there is no physical product.

Revisions

  • You get 2 rounds of changes on the commissioned piece included in the price.
  • A fee will be added if you want something changed on the final drawing - unless a misunderstanding from my side has been made.
  • If you wish for me to change the subject in the drawing you have previously approved, I will charge you a fee to change it. The amount of the extra charge depends on the change you want me to make and is usually based on an hourly wage.
  • If your reference is unclear to start with changes will come at an extra charge.

What Is The Process?

  1. Form: Fill out the commission form on the commission page
  2. Quote: When you’ve received your slot by email, you will get a free quote.
  3. Price: When you’ve agreed to the price, we can continue to the next step. We will look over the references you’ve sent. If they are not sufficient (they are not clear), you may have to find more; if you can’t for whatever reason, I can find them for you based on your description for a small fee.
  4. Draft and Mockups: You must decide which Drafts/Mockups I should continue with (depending on the complexity of the commission there might be fewer or more drafts.)
  5. Final Sketch: You must accept the final sketches that you are happy with, if not, you have two free revisions. If you accept and want changes after, you have to pay an extra fee.
  6. Color Choice: Insight and involvement in color plan (depending on the complexity of the commissioned piece.)
  7. Deliverable: You (the commissioner) will receive two files. One file compatible with the internet (.jpeg) and one full-resolution file (.png).
