Commission Form

Please fill out the form with the appropriate fields. Choose between a category: character or an environment. Choose the style: sketch, lineart, lineart & color, or painterly. Choose the layout: portrait or landscape. Adding a background to a character is extra. Adding characters to the environment or more characters to the 'character sheet' is extra. If asking for additions, please fill out the message box regarding extra additions. Please link at least 5 references (max 10 images)(you may link a Google Drive containing the photos as well). In the comments please describe what you want for your commission. I will let you know if you made the slot, and what number you are. If you don't receive a response, within the week, you either entered your email wrong or you didn't make the slot list. Please read the terms of service!
